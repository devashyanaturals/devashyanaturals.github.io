✦ From Hive to Home

How we harvest with care

Full transparency, every step. Watch our videos to see exactly how your honey is made.

1
🔍 Locating Wild Hives

We work with forest trackers to identify wild bee colonies deep in unpolluted zones, far from farms and chemicals.

2
🔥 Traditional Smoke Method

Natural smoke from herbs calms the bees gently — a technique passed down through generations that harms no one.

3
🧺 Careful Comb Extraction

Honeycomb sections are removed by hand. We always leave plenty behind so the colony continues thriving.

4
❄️ Cold Processing

Honey is extracted at ambient temperature — never heated — preserving all live enzymes, pollen, and antioxidants.

5
🫙 Pack & Dispatch

One light strain removes debris. Packed fresh into glass jars, sealed, and shipped to your door within days.
