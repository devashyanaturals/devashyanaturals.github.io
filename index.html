✶ From Hive to Home

How we harvest with care

Full transparency at every step. Watch our videos to see exactly how your honey is made.

1
🔍 Locating Wild Hives

We work with forest trackers to find wild bee colonies deep in unpolluted zones, far from farms and chemicals.

2
🔥 Traditional Smoke Method

Natural smoke from herbs calms the bees gently, a technique passed down through generations that harms no one.

3
🧺 Careful Comb Extraction

Honeycomb sections removed by hand. We always leave plenty behind so the colony continues to thrive.

4
❄ Cold Processing

Honey extracted at ambient temperature, never heated, preserving all live enzymes, pollen, and antioxidants.

5
🫙 Pack & Dispatch

One light strain removes debris. Packed into glass jars, sealed tight, and shipped to your door within days.
